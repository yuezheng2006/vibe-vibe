## Access & accounts (FAQ)

- **Do I need an Anthropic / claude.ai account for the course?** Courses run on Skilljar; you typically only need a **Skilljar** account. For ongoing Claude product use, sign up separately at [claude.ai](https://claude.ai).
- **Privacy**: Progress, completion, quizzes, and time-on-page may be logged; see Anthropic’s course FAQ and [Skilljar’s Privacy Policy](https://www.skilljar.com/privacy).
- **Data deletion**: Email [academy-support@anthropic.com](mailto:academy-support@anthropic.com) (subject to policy and law).
